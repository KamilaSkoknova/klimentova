Psychotherapy

Continue your journey with support

My approach is deep, psychodynamic, and highly sensitive to each client’s experience. I don’t offer quick fixes or ready-made answers – I believe in the process where a person can gradually discover what’s holding them back, what empowers them, and what brings meaning. Together, we search for inner motivation – not the external, imposed kind, but the one that springs from a deep, inner desire to change one’s life and take back control.

"There is always something you can do and succeed at. Where there is life, there is hope."

This therapeutic motto is not just a phrase – it’s a foundation of my work. I believe every person carries within the potential to change, grow, and make new choices. Even when life feels chaotic, therapy offers a safe space where clarity can begin to emerge. And from that clarity, new motivation can grow – one that is genuine, lasting, and truly yours.

About me

Mgr. Ivana Klimentová, PhD.

I studied social work at Matej Bel University and hold a PhD in social policy, specializing in social counseling and social services from the University of St. Cyril and Methodius. In my practice, I focus primarily on guiding and supporting people from marginalized communities, including LGBTI individuals and women and children experiencing violence.

I have worked in various social services such as the National Helpline for Women Experiencing Violence, women's shelters, and counseling centers for victims of violence. I’ve also been part of several working groups supporting children from re-education centers as well as people in prison.

In my work, I rely on a trauma-sensitive approach and a deep psychodynamic orientation that explores early childhood and adolescent experiences.

It would be my honor and joy to walk with you through the journey of healing and discovery.

I offer sessions in English. I am here to support you on your journey of self-discovery and personal growth.
